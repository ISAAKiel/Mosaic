4. Data Frames, Martices & Arrays | Matrices

A Matrix stores multiple elements in a two dimensional array.

mat <- matrix(vec_a, nrow=2)        # matrix(data, nrow = rows, ncol = cols)
mat
##      [,1] [,2] [,3] [,4]
## [1,]    3    2    5    5
## [2,]    5    2    8    2
mat_t <- t(mat)
mat_t
##      [,1] [,2]
## [1,]    3    5
## [2,]    2    2
## [3,]    5    8
## [4,]    5    2
.
